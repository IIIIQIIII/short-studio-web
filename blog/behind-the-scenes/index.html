Behind the Scenes: Making of 'I Was a Stepmother in the 80s'

Dive into the creative process behind our stop-motion animation masterpiece. From character design to final animation, see how we brought this 80s drama to life using AI-powered tools.

Step 1: Character Design

First, we generated the male and female lead character designs through style descriptions. These foundational images captured the essence of our 80s protagonists.

Step 2: Scene Generation

Next, based on the indoor room settings, we created the image of the characters interacting in the room. This scene depicted their housework moment, setting the stage for emotional exchange.

Step 3: Animation and Storyboarding

Finally, through real actor performances, we brought the storyboard images to life. Watch the final animated sequence that combines all elements into a dynamic short drama clip.

This process showcases the innovative blend of AI generation and traditional animation techniques, resulting in a captivating piece that resonates with modern audiences while evoking 80s nostalgia.
